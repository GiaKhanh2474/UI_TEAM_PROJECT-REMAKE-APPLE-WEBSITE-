Designed with
the earth in mind.
Every Apple display has been carefully designed to minimize its environmental impact. The enclosures of both Studio Display and Pro Display XDR are made with low‑carbon aluminum, and the solder of the logic boards uses 100 percent recycled tin. Both displays are ENERGY STAR® certified2 and free of numerous harmful substances, like mercury and PVC. All the virgin wood fiber in our packaging comes from responsibly managed forests.
